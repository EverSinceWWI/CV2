ABOUT ME

Curious about multimedia technologies since I was a child, I’ve found myself fascinated by the concept of videogames, more than videogames themselves. This passion brought me to develop an interest in the interaction design field, especially that related to entertainment. During my master degree I discovered UX Design, a design field that got me thrilled ever since the first map made. My technical knowledge ranges from graphic design to video and audio editing, and in the last years I decided to focus my attention mainly on prototyping and programming for design. The projects realized during my bachelor and master degree allowed me to put my previous knowledge into practice, and acquire even more competences based on the necessities of each project.
